Winter 2019

We are currently accepting applications for January 2019 hiring cycle. It will take place in New York, NY for a duration of 14 months. 50 engineers will be selected. Please read the rules before submitting.

  1. You must be at least 18 years old and able to work in the United States legally.
  2. Applications must be received on December 1st no later than 8pm Eastern Standard Time.
  3. Selected individuals will be notified via email.
  4. There will be an in person interview for the selected candidates.
  5. Official acceptance will be sent via email no later than December 15th.
  6. Room and board will be available for candidates starting December 26th.
  7. Mentors will be meet and greet engineers on January 10th, 2019.
